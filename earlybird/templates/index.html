Welcome to EarlyBird

Your gateway to sentiment analysis on Reddit

Introducing EarlyBird, our innovative Reddit sentiment analyzer application designed to offer users a unique insight into the emotions behind Reddit posts and comments. EarlyBird provides a seamless user experience, allowing users to sign up or log in with ease. After logging in, users are directed to a search page where they can enter a Reddit username to dive into the world of sentiment analysis. Our application fetches the titles of all the posts made by the specified user, analyzing them through our advanced machine learning model, trained with Sentiment140.

Each post title is assessed based on its sentiment score, with values greater than 0.5 deemed positive and those less than 0.5 considered negative. These titles are then displayed, making them clickable for users to explore further. Upon clicking a title, EarlyBird fetches all comments related to the post, running them through the sentiment analysis model as well. The comments are similarly divided into positive and negative categories, providing a comprehensive view of the sentiments expressed in the Reddit post and its subsequent discussion.

EarlyBird's visually appealing interface presents the positive and negative titles and comments in two separate columns, with positive sentiments highlighted in green and negative sentiments in red. This intuitive color-coding system allows users to quickly identify and understand the prevailing emotions behind the content they are viewing. Whether you're a casual Reddit user or a business seeking to analyze customer feedback, EarlyBird is the perfect tool for gaining valuable insights into the sentiments expressed on one of the world's largest online platforms. Discover the power of sentiment analysis with EarlyBird and unlock a new perspective on Reddit content today.
